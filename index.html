About Me

Hello! I'm Sasha, the creator behind SashArt — a brand built with passion for creativity and expression. At SashArt, I offer a variety of artistic services including art commissions, Live2D model creation, design commissions, and website development. I also handcraft custom bead bracelets and phone strap charms, perfect for adding a personal touch to your everyday style.

Whether it's digital or handmade, every project I take on is made with care, detail, and love for art. My mission is to help others bring their ideas to life — one piece at a time.

Feel free to check out my work, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to collaborate!
