About Me

Hello! I'm Sasha, the creator behind SashArt — a brand built with passion for creativity and expression. At SashArt, I offer a variety of artistic services including art commissions, Live2D model creation, design commissions, and website development. I also handcraft custom bead bracelets and phone strap charms, perfect for adding a personal touch to your everyday style.

Whether it's digital or handmade, every project I take on is made with care, detail, and love for art. My mission is to help others bring their ideas to life — one piece at a time.

Feel free to check out my work, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to collaborate!

Services

Art Commissions

Custom digital illustrations tailored to your vision. Whether it's original characters, portraits, or fan art, every piece is drawn with attention to detail and creative passion. Live2D model commissions are also available for VTubers and creators looking to bring their characters to life.

Beads Collections

Handcrafted bead bracelets and phone straps made with love and care. Choose from our ready-made collections or request a custom design that reflects your personality, mood, or aesthetic. Perfect as gifts or personal accessories.

Design & Web Services

From UI/UX design to full website development, we provide clean, modern, and responsive designs for your personal or business needs. We also offer brand design, logos, and layouts to help you establish a strong visual identity online.
